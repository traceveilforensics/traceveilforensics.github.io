This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Trace Veil Forensics ("Provider") and the client ("Client") for cybersecurity consulting services.

2. Services

Provider delivers remote cybersecurity consulting services including but not limited to:

  • Security assessments and vulnerability scanning
  • Incident response and digital forensics
  • IT solutions and infrastructure support
  • Security training and awareness programs

3. Pricing Structure

Security Services

  • Security Assessments: Starting at KES 7,500 per project
  • Vulnerability Scanning: Starting at KES 3,000 per scan
  • Incident Response: KES 12,500 per incident or KES 2,500/hour
  • Digital Forensics: KES 15,000 per case or KES 2,500/hour

IT Solutions

  • IT Support: KES 5,000 per month retainer or KES 1,500/hour
  • Network Setup: KES 3,000 per network
  • Data Backup: KES 4,000 setup

Training Programs

  • Security Awareness: KES 2,500-7,500 per session
  • Custom Training: Pricing based on scope and duration

Retainer Options: Monthly retainers available for ongoing support at discounted rates.

Travel: For on-site work, travel costs may apply in addition to service fees.

4. Payment Terms

  • 50% deposit required before work begins
  • Balance due upon completion or monthly for retainers
  • Payment via M-Pesa or bank transfer
  • Invoices sent electronically

5. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to protect confidential information shared during the engagement. This includes:

  • Client systems, data, and security configurations
  • Assessment findings and reports
  • Business and technical information

This obligation continues for 2 years after the engagement ends.

6. Scope of Work

Work scope is defined in project proposals or statements of work. Provider will:

  • Deliver services as outlined in the agreed scope
  • Communicate progress and any scope changes
  • Provide written reports and recommendations

Client will:

  • Provide access to systems as needed
  • Respond to inquiries promptly
  • Make decisions on recommendations within reasonable time

Provider warrants:

Client pays for work completed up to termination date.

Immediate termination possible for breach of agreement or illegal activity.
