Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our cybersecurity services

What services does Trace Veil Forensics offer?

We provide comprehensive cybersecurity services including security assessments, vulnerability scanning, incident response, digital forensics, IT solutions, and security awareness training. All services are delivered remotely to clients worldwide.

How much do your services cost?

We offer competitive pricing designed for small and medium businesses. Our assessment packages start at affordable rates, and we provide clear quotes upfront before any work begins. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

How quickly can you respond to security incidents?

We understand that security incidents require urgent attention. We strive to respond within hours, not days. Our remote-first model allows us to start working on your incident immediately without travel delays.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Yes! We work with startups, small businesses, and medium-sized enterprises. Our services are scalable and priced to be accessible to businesses at various stages of growth. We tailor our approach to match your specific security needs and budget.

Where are you located and do you work remotely?

We're based in Kenya and serve clients globally through our remote delivery model. This means we can work with businesses anywhere in the world without the added costs and delays of on-site visits. We use secure communication channels and can coordinate with your team across any timezone.

What makes Trace Veil Forensics different from other security firms?

We combine enterprise-grade security expertise with pricing that makes sense for smaller businesses. Unlike large firms with expensive overhead, we operate lean and remote-first, passing those savings to you. You work directly with experienced consultants, not junior staff or account managers.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out via email or phone. We'll schedule a brief consultation to understand your needs and recommend the right services for your situation. From there, we'll provide a clear quote and timeline for the work.
