Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your personal information and maintaining your privacy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Effective Date: January 3, 2026

1. Who We Are

Trace Veil Forensics is a small cybersecurity startup based in Kenya. We provide security assessments, vulnerability scanning, incident response, and digital forensics services remotely to clients worldwide.

This policy explains how we handle your information because, as a security company, we practice what we preach.

2. Information You Share With Us

When you reach out through our website, we collect:

  • Contact details - Your name, email, phone, and company
  • Your needs - Information about what security help you're looking for

We don't collect anything we don't need. No hidden tracking, no unnecessary data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for one reason only: to help you with your cybersecurity needs.

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide the services you hire us for
  • Improve our website based on visitor feedback

We don't send spam. We don't share your data with marketers.

4. Do We Share Your Data?

No. Your information stays with us.

The only exceptions:

  • Service providers - Like EmailJS (who sends our emails) - they only see what's needed to deliver the service
  • Legal requirements - If forced by law, we'll disclose what's required

5. How We Protect Your Data

As security professionals, we take protection seriously:

  • Our website uses HTTPS encryption
  • We limit access to client information to essential personnel
  • We review our practices regularly

Important: While we protect your data, no system is 100% foolproof. If a breach occurs despite our efforts, we'll notify you promptly.

6. Client Confidentiality

When you hire us for security work, we sign NDAs when requested. Your assessment results, findings, and any sensitive data remain confidential unless you authorize release.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information we have about you
  • Ask us to correct wrong information
  • Ask us to delete your information
  • Unsubscribe from any communications

Email us at traceveilforensics@gmail.com to make a request.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies - mainly to remember dark mode preference. You can disable cookies in your browser without affecting functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

As a growing startup, we may update this policy. We'll post changes here with the new date. For significant changes, we'll try to notify existing contacts.
